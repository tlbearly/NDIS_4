Wildfire Layers

The wildfire map layers are maintained and imported on demand from ESRI's USA Current Wildfires layer. They present the best-known point and perimeter locations of wildfire occurrences within the United States over the past 7 days from IRWIN and NIFC information.

Wildfire Incidents: Location of wildfire occurrences over the past 7 days.

Wildfire Perimeters: Perimeter of wildfire occurrences over the past 7 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there any way to get a downloadable version of the Colorado HB1298 Habitat Impact Assessment Tool?
A: No, the Habitat Impact Assessment Tool is a web based application and can not be downloaded.

Q: What about the data? Can you provide me with the Colorado HB1298 Habitat Impact Assessment Tool data?
A: You can find most of our data on ArcGIS.com by searching 'GROUPS' for 'Colorado Parks and Wildlife' or by following this link, Colorado Parks and Wildlife on ArcGIS.com.

For additional questions about Colorado Parks and Wildlife please submit your question to AskCPW.

For local Parks and Wildlife office locations and contact information, please use the Colorado Parks and Wildlife's Office Directory.
